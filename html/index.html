This is the door mat to the Göktuğ’s homely-page, I’m a '90s kid from Turkey™, an MA student in Linguistics at Hacettepe University and an hobbyist itch-your-own-scratch hacker.

Most of the content on this server is found in my Gemini capsule. Gemini is a new Internet protocol which puts people and text at the centre, and it’s truly a fresh breeze considering what WWW has become. You can view cadadr.space through an online proxy, but I highly suggest you install one of the many browsers available. My favourites are Lagrange for desktop, and Deedum on Android.

An HTML copy of of the Gemini capsule is available at wormhole/.

My main WWW site is at gkayaalp.com. I also have a seldom updated gopherhole on SDF.ORG.

The will soon be a CGit instance on this server, till then the canonical location for my Git repositories is Microsoft Github.

If you want to contact me, shoot me a plain text, UTF-8 email, or @ me at @cadadr@mastodon.sdf.org.

Made with mellow boredom in Istanbul, with part of my ❤ in Ankara.
